## 简体中文

为了让 Where My Project 保持一个友好、高效、可协作的社区环境，参与者都应遵守以下基本规则。

### 我们鼓励

- 尊重不同背景、经验和观点
- 针对问题讨论问题，保持建设性
- 接受并提供清晰、具体、善意的反馈
- 在提出异议时，尽量给出上下文、复现方式或改进建议

### 我们不接受

- 人身攻击、羞辱、挑衅或骚扰
- 歧视性语言，或让他人感到被排斥的表达
- 恶意刷屏、钓鱼、破坏讨论秩序
- 故意发布明显无关、误导或破坏性的内容

### 执行方式

维护者可以删除不合适的内容，并视情况关闭讨论、拒绝贡献或限制参与。

如果你发现不合适的行为，请通过 GitHub Issue 或 Pull Request 讨论区之外的合适方式联系维护者处理。

## English

To keep Where My Project welcoming and productive, all participants are expected to follow these basic community rules.

### Expected Behavior

- Respect people with different backgrounds, experience levels, and opinions
- Keep discussion constructive and focused on the problem
- Give and receive clear, specific, good-faith feedback
- When disagreeing, provide context, reproduction steps, or concrete alternatives when possible

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, intimidation, or abuse
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Knowingly posting misleading, irrelevant, or destructive content

### Enforcement

Maintainers may remove inappropriate content and, when needed, close discussions, reject contributions, or limit participation.

If you observe harmful behavior, please contact the maintainers through an appropriate private channel instead of escalating the conflict in public discussion.
